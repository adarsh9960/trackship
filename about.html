Our Journey Through Time

Milestones That Marked Our Growth

2018

The Beginning

Trackship Logistics founded in Mumbai with a small team of 5 members. Started operations with 2 delivery vans serving local businesses in Mumbai Metropolitan Region.

2019

First Major Expansion

Expanded operations to 5 major cities - Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, and Hyderabad. Launched our proprietary tracking system and introduced B2B delivery solutions.

2020

Technology Leap

Deployed AI-powered route optimization system, reducing delivery times by 30%. Despite pandemic challenges, successfully served 500+ COVID-essential deliveries daily.

2021

Franchise Launch

Launched our franchise program, establishing 25 franchise points across India. Introduced electric delivery vehicles in metro cities, beginning our sustainability journey.

2022

Reaching 50+ Cities

Achieved milestone of 50,000+ daily deliveries. Launched B2B Seller Platform and integrated with major e-commerce platforms. Received "Fastest Growing Logistics Company" award.

2023

National Leadership

Expanded to 750+ cities with 75+ franchise partners. Launched advanced analytics dashboard for clients. Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality management.

2024

Innovation & Excellence

Introduced drone delivery trials in selected zones. Launched mobile app with real-time tracking features. Reached 500+ team members and set new industry standards for service excellence.

Making a Difference Beyond Business

Our Commitment to Social Responsibility

At Trackship Logistics, we believe that true success extends beyond business metrics. Our commitment to corporate social responsibility reflects our dedication to creating positive change in the communities we serve and protecting our environment for future generations.

Our Green Logistics Initiative has led to the adoption of 200+ electric delivery vehicles in metro cities, reducing our carbon footprint by 40% over the past two years. We've implemented paperless billing systems and optimized route planning to minimize fuel consumption, setting new standards for sustainable logistics in India.

Through our Education Support Program, we've provided educational assistance to over 500 underprivileged students across India. Our Skill Development Initiative offers free logistics and delivery training to youth from economically weaker sections, creating employment opportunities and empowering communities.

During natural disasters and emergencies, our Rapid Response Team provides free delivery services for essential supplies. We've partnered with NGOs and government agencies to ensure critical reach, including medical supplies during COVID-19, relief materials during floods, and educational materials in remote areas.
