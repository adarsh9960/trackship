Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Our Services

What is the investment required for a Trackship Courier Point franchise?

The investment for a Trackship Courier Point franchise starts from ₹5 lakhs and can go up to ₹15 lakhs depending on the location and scale of operations. This includes setup costs, initial inventory, and working capital. We also offer flexible financing options and EMI plans to help deserving candidates start their business.

How long does it take to start operations after signing up for a franchise?

Typically, it takes 15-30 days to complete the setup and start operations. This time includes location verification, store setup, training completion, and system integration. Our dedicated franchise support team ensures a smooth and quick onboarding process.

What kind of support does Trackship provide to franchise partners?

Trackship provides comprehensive support including initial training, ongoing operational guidance, marketing assistance, technology platform access, customer service support, and regular performance reviews. We also conduct periodic training programs and workshops to keep our franchise partners updated with the latest industry practices.

Can I integrate Trackship's logistics services with my existing business systems?

Yes, Trackship offers seamless API integration with various business systems including ERP, e-commerce platforms, inventory management systems, and CRM software. Our technical team provides complete support for integration and customization based on your specific business requirements.

What makes Trackship's B2B services different from competitors?

Trackship's B2B services stand out due to our technology-first approach, flexible pricing models, dedicated account managers, customized solutions, and nationwide network. We offer end-to-end visibility, real-time tracking, and analytics dashboards that provide complete control over your logistics operations.

How does Trackship ensure package safety and security?

We implement multiple security measures including GPS-enabled vehicles, real-time tracking, trained delivery personnel, secure packaging guidelines, insurance options, and 24/7 monitoring. Our quality assurance processes and regular audits ensure consistent service quality and package safety.

What are the future services Trackship is planning to launch?

Trackship is actively developing solar energy solutions, EV charging infrastructure, advanced technology platforms, drone delivery services, automated logistics centers, and AI-powered customer service systems. We're also exploring international logistics partnerships and blockchain-based supply chain solutions.

How can I track my shipments with Trackship?

You can track your shipments through our website, mobile app, WhatsApp bot, or SMS tracking system. Simply enter your tracking number or order ID to get real-time updates including current location, estimated delivery time, and delivery confirmation. We also provide email and SMS notifications at key delivery milestones.
